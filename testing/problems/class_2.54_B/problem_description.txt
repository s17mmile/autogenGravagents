Problem Description:
A potato of mass 0.5 kg moves under Earth's gravity with an air resistive force of -$kmv$. The terminal velocity of the potato when released from rest is $v = 1000$ m/s, with $k=0.01s^{-1}$. Find the maximum height of the potato if it has the same value of k, but it is initially shot directly upward with a student-made potato gun with an initial velocity of $120$ m/s.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Reference Explanation:
None

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Correct Final Answer:
680 $m$
