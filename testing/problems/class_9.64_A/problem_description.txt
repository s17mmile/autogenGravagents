Problem Description:
A new single-stage rocket is developed in the year 2023, having a gas exhaust velocity of $4000$ m/s. The total mass of the rocket is $10^5$ kg, with $90$% of its mass being fuel. The fuel burns quickly in $100$ s at a constant rate. For testing purposes, the rocket is launched vertically at rest from Earth's surface. Neglect air resistance and assume that the acceleration of gravity is constant. Determine how high the launched object can reach above the surface of Earth.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Reference Explanation:
None

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Correct Final Answer:
3700 km
