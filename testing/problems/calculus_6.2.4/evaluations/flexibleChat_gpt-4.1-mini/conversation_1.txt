A cross-section in the plane $P_x$ has the shape of a washer (an annular ring) with inner radius $x^2$ and outer radius $x$, so we find the cross-sectional area by subtracting the area of the inner circle from the area of the outer circle:
$$
A(x)=\pi x^2-\pi\left(x^2\right)^2=\pi\left(x^2-x^4\right)
$$
Therefore we have
$$
\begin{aligned}
V & =\int_0^1 A(x) d x=\int_0^1 \pi\left(x^2-x^4\right) d x \\
& =\pi\left[\frac{x^3}{3}-\frac{x^5}{5}\right]_0^1=\frac{2 \pi}{15}
\end{aligned}
$$
