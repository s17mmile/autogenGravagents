Problem Description:
A particle of charge $-q_1$ is at the origin of an $x$ axis. At what location on the axis should a particle of charge $-4 q_1$ be placed so that the net electric field is zero at $x=2.0 \mathrm{~mm}$ on the axis? 

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Reference Explanation:


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Correct Final Answer:
6.0 $\mathrm{~mm}$
