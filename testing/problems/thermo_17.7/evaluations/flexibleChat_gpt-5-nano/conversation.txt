= & 2.76 \times 10^{-6} \mathrm{~m}^3 \mathrm{~s}^{-1}
\end{aligned}
$$

\begin{aligned}
n_{\mathrm{CO}_2} & =22.7 \mathrm{~kg}\left(\frac{1}{0.044 \mathrm{~kg} \mathrm{~mol}^{-1}}\right)=516 \mathrm{~mol} \\

\end{aligned}
$$

$$

$$
