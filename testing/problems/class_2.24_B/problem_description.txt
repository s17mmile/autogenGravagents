Problem Description:
A skier weighing $90$ kg starts from rest down a hill inclined at $17^\circ$. He skis 100 m down the hill and then coasts for 70 m along level snow until he stops. Given a coefficient of kinetic friction of $\mu_k = 0.18$, what velocity does the skier have at the bottom of the hill?

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Reference Explanation:
None

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Correct Final Answer:
15.6 $m/s$
