Problem Description:

Estimate the additional non-expansion work that may be obtained by raising the temperature to blood temperature, $37^{\circ} \mathrm{C}$.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Reference Explanation:


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Correct Final Answer:
-21 $\mathrm{kJ} \mathrm{mol}^{-1}$
