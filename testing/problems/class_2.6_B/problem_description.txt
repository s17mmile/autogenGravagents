Problem Description:
In the blizzard of ' 88 , a rancher was forced to drop hay bales from an airplane to feed her cattle. The plane flew horizontally at $160 \mathrm{~km} / \mathrm{hr}$ and dropped the bales from a height of $80 \mathrm{~m}$ above the flat range. To not hit the cattle, what is the largest time error she could make while pushing the bales out of the airplane? Ignore air resistance.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Reference Explanation:
None

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Correct Final Answer:
0.68 seconds
