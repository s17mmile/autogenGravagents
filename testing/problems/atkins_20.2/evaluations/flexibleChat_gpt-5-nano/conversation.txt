The mass loss $\Delta m$ in an interval $\Delta t$ is related to the collision flux by
$$
\Delta m=Z_{\mathrm{W}} A_0 m \Delta t
$$

$$
Z_{\mathrm{W}}=\frac{\Delta m}{A_0 m \Delta t}
$$
