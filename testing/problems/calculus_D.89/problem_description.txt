Problem Description:
Find the area of triangle $A B C$, correct to five decimal places, if
$$
|A B|=10 \mathrm{~cm} \quad|B C|=3 \mathrm{~cm} \quad \angle A B C=107^{\circ}
$$

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Reference Explanation:


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Correct Final Answer:
14.34457  $\mathrm{cm^2}$
