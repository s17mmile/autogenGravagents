--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

$\begin{array}{lllll}T / \mathrm{K} & 350 & 400 & 450 & 500 \\ \left(10^3 \mathrm{~K}\right) / T & 2.86 & 2.50 & 2.22 & 2.00 \\ -\ln K & 7.83 & 4.26 & 1.68 & -0.39\end{array}$

$$
\Delta_{\mathrm{r}} H^{\ominus}=\left(+9.6 \times 10^3 \mathrm{~K}\right) \times R=+80 \mathrm{~kJ} \mathrm{~mol}^{-1}
$$


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
