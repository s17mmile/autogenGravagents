$$
T / K=20+273.15, \text { so } T=(20+273.15) \mathrm{K}
$$

$$

1 \mathrm{Jm}^{-3} & =1 \mathrm{~Pa} \\
& \stackrel{\infty}{=} 4.35 \times 10^5 \mathrm{~Pa}=435 \mathrm{kPa}
\end{aligned}
