Problem Description:
A free neutron is unstable and decays into a proton and an electron. How much energy other than the rest energies of the proton and electron is available if a neutron at rest decays? (This is an example of nuclear beta decay. Another particle, called a neutrino-- actually an antineutrino $\bar v$ is also produced.)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Reference Explanation:
None

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Final Answer:
0.8 $MeV$
