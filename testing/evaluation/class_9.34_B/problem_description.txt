Problem Description:
A billiard ball of intial velocity $u_1$ collides with another billard ball (same mass) initially at rest. The first ball moves off at $\psi = 45^\circ$. For an elastic collision, say the velocities of both balls after the collision is $v_1 = v_2 = \frac{u_1}{\sqrt(2)}$. At what LAB angle does the second ball emerge?

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Reference Explanation:
None

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Final Answer:
45 $^\circ$
