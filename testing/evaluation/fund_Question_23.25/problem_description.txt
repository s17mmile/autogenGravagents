Problem Description:
An infinite line of charge produces a field of magnitude $4.5 \times$ $10^4 \mathrm{~N} / \mathrm{C}$ at distance $2.0 \mathrm{~m}$. Find the linear charge density.


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Reference Explanation:


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Final Answer:
5.0 $\mu \mathrm{C} / \mathrm{m}$
